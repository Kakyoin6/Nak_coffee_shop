Our Nakuru Story

Founded in 2020, Nakuru Coffee House brings premium coffee culture to the heart of Kenya's Rift Valley. We source our beans directly from local Kenyan farms, supporting our community while delivering exceptional coffee.

Located in beautiful Nakuru city, we roast our beans in-house to bring out the unique flavors and aromas that Kenyan AA coffee is famous for - bright acidity, wine-like flavor notes, and rich body.

Whether you're a local resident, tourist visiting Lake Nakuru, or business professional, our coffee house offers the perfect blend of Kenyan hospitality and world-class coffee.
